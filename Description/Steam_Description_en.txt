Adds the [b]Frontier Town Quarter[/b] district that allows working tiles in the fourth and fifth city ring.

[img]https://i.imgur.com/VogbSSm.gif[/img]


[h1]Details[/h1]
[list][*]This isn’t a specialty district, so it doesn’t require population.
[*]Tiles in the outer ring can be worked just like those in the inner three rings.
[*]Wonders or districts cannot be placed in the outer rings.
[*]Tiles in the outer rings can now be swapped.[/list]

[quote][h2][img]https://i.ibb.co/Y799qWs/District-Wor-Text-en.png[/img][/h2]
[i]District (Medieval Era - Feudalism)[/i]

Citizens can work tiles in the fourth ring of this city.
Tiles in the fourth ring of this city can be purchased by Gold. 
Culture bombs adjacent neutral tiles. Culture bombs can annex tiles in the fourth ring of this city.

+1 Housing
[i]Cannot be adjacent to a City Center.[/i][/quote]

[quote][h2][img]https://i.ibb.co/k1Mpk8x/Building-LS-Text-en.png[/img][/h2]
[i]Building (Industrial Era - Urbanization)[/i]

Citizens can work tiles in the fifth ring of this city.
Tiles in the fifth ring of this city can be purchased by Gold.
Railroad bombs adjacent owned tiles if the technology Steam Power is researched at the time of completion. Culture bombs can annex tiles in the fifth ring of this city.

[i]Requires: Frontier Town Quarter[/i][/quote]


[h1]Known Issues & How To Contribute?[/h1]
[list][*]Worked tiles in the outer rings currently lack animations, such as spinning windmills. If you have experience with [b]improvement 3D modding[/b] and would like to contribute, please get in touch with us.
[*]Mountain tiles in outer rings acquired through purchase, swapping, or culture bombs won’t apply modifiers (such as Inca production) until reloading the game.[/list]
Please report bugs or suggest features here:
